THE SPORTS CAR REDEFINED: THE BMW i8.

Progressive driving pleasure.The BMW i8 is ready to revolutionise its vehicle class. As the first sports car with the consumption and emission values of a compact car. The strength of the plug-in hybrid lies, among other factors, in the perfect synchronisation of electric motor and combustion engine, which makes itself apparent in maximum efficiency and dynamics on the road. The first sports car that even accelerates the zeitgeist.

Previous Next

  • Design

    With its fascinating holistic concept, the BMW i8 brings the vision of a modern and sustainable sports car to life. The highly-emotional design immediately makes it clear what qualities the BMW i8 has to offer. Dynamic proportions show off its acceleration potential even when it’s standing still; and yet it doesn’t rely on being a pure-blooded sports car. Its aesthetics always follow a functional goal: for example, more aerodynamics for maximum efficiency and absolute performance.

  • Drive & efficiency

    More than just sporty. The BMW i8 revolutionised sporty mobility with its unique drive concept. It marked a new level of evolution for BMW EfficientDynamics technology. Its plug-in hybrid concept and the combination of three-cylinder petrol engine and LifeDrive architecture specific to BMW i ensure a fascinating driving experience. And prove that dynamics and efficiency aren’t opposites, but can be experienced simultaneously.

  • LED light design with laser light.

    The optional high-beam headlights with laser light technology increases the range of the high beam and enhances its illumination. Using a fraction of the energy of conventional headlights

FASCINATION BMW M.

Passion for performance. High performance, outstanding technology and the true sensation of motor sports can be expressed in one single letter: M. The strongest letter in the world.

Previous Next

  • Driving Dynamics.

    431 hp are at your command. And when the M TwinPower Turbo 6-cylinder petrol engine accelerates to the limit in next to no time, you immediately realise just what the BMW M4 Coupé is capable of. The boost stays finely adjustable even in the highest rev ranges. Adapted motor sport technologies like the Active M differential bring the power of the M drive train to the street and ensure maximum safety and controllability in any situation.

  • Design.

    High performance at a glance: the BMW M4 Coupé continues the tradition of M automobiles and unites a powerfully sporty look with spectacular design. Its athletic lines and elongated silhouette strengthen the appearance and dynamics of this racing car icon. The interior blends in seamlessly: every detail is coordinated around the driver, and boasts the best possible control and uncompromising motor sport character.

  • Efficiency.

    As in other areas, the BMW M4 Coupé is a top performer when it comes to efficiency, too. Intelligent EfficientDynamics measures such as the Auto Start-Stop function or Brake Energy Regeneration ensure optimal use of resources and thus play a crucial role in maintaining the power and stamina of the BMW M4 Coupé. This results in not only better dynamics, but also a reduction in fuel consumption and emissions.
